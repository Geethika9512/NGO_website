About Plantevo

Planatova is a nature-driven non-profit organization committed to restoring ecological balance, promoting green lifestyles, and inspiring sustainable living. Founded with a vision to reconnect humanity with nature, we believe that even a small plant can spark big change.
🌱 Mission: To protect, restore, and nurture the environment through community-driven actions, education, and innovation.
